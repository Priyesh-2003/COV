Welcome to Confederation of Valuers

Confederation of Valuers is a premier professional body dedicated to advancing the valuation profession in India and globally. As a Section 8 not-for-profit institution, we uphold international valuation standards, ethical practices, and professional development. Our structured membership pathways empower individuals and organizations to grow, collaborate, and lead in the valuation industry.
